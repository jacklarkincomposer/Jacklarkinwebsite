About Me

I'm Jack, a composer and sound designer currently in my final year at the University of Huddersfield studying Screen Composition and Sound Design. Over the past five years I've written music for online content creators, independent game teams and short film productions, working closely with directors and creators complementing their projects with bespoke soundtracks.

My writing spans many different areas of music, from ambient, world music and hybrid orchestral/electronic styles, with a particular passion for cinematic scoring. I've always been drawn to the way music can completely transform moving images, and that fascination with atmosphere and storytelling is what inspired me towards screen composition. I work from brief to delivery, taking feedback seriously and adapting quickly to a project's needs. I am comfortable working in many different styles with a keen ear for attention to detail and always open to explore new areas.

Alongside composition I have a strong background in sound design, giving me an understanding of how music and audio sit together in a final mix. I'm always looking to push into new genres and styles.
