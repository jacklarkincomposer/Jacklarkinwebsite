A Fictional World — Original Soundtrack

The AncientWanderer

An unnamed traveller. A world without edges. A silence that remembers.

✦ ✦ ✦

Track I

The Summit at the Edge of Memory

Sparse · Lost · Searching

The traveller arrives at a height that has no name. Cloud moves through stone. Below, a world stretches vast and indifferent — magnificent, untouched, unknowable. There is no path forward. There is only the wind, and the question it carries.

Solo String
Harp

A world remembered before it was ever known.

Track II

The Valley of Forgotten Names

Ancient · Warm · Melancholic

Descending from the heights, the traveller finds evidence of lives once lived. Stone walls half-claimed by earth. A fire pit cold for a thousand years. Folk melodies rise here — not sad, but weighted with time, carrying the ghost of people who sang them.

Folk
Light Perc

Track III — IV

The World Revealed

Epic · Triumphant · Ancient

The full weight of the world arrives. From the main theme — rebuilt, reborn — every motif that has been quietly present now sounds in full. Brass. Choir. The percussion of a civilisation that once shook the earth. This is the heartbeat that all other tracks orbit.

Cello
Light Orc

Every silence was simply the world gathering its breath.

Track V

Where the Sea Holds Its Dead

Dark · Reverent · Still

A coastline where temples crumble into black water. The traveller finds beauty in ruins — not tragedy, but completion. The themes from the world's heartbeat return here, stripped to their bones. A single voice where once a thousand sang.

Double Bass
Choir

Track VI

The Road That Leads to Itself

Resolved · Distant · Eternal

The journey does not end — it becomes circular. The traveller stands again at a threshold, but now carries the weight of every place visited. The world's main theme returns one final time — transformed, quieter, older. The last sound is the first sound. The circle holds.
